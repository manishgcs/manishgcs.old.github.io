I am a 4th year doctoral student working with Prof. Parasara Sridhar Duggirala at Computer Science Department at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.

The research is focused on Cyber-Physical Systems, their verification and validation using formal methods as well as neural networks.

Prior to joining grad school, I have worked in Kernel group for SoC verification platform, SpyGlass, at Synopsys India Pvt. Ltd. I have also worked with Prof. Goran Frehse in Parsers team for affine linear hybrid systems verification platform, SpaceEx, at Verimag Research Lab>, Grenoble.
